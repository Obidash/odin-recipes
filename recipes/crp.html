Cinnamon Roll Pancakes

Description

This recipe will take you through some steps to make some super delicious vegan cinnamon roll pancakes! If you have ever wanted some fluffly, mouth wateringly tasting, and aesthetically appealing pancakes, well then you're in for a treat! This recipe makes 12 big pancakes, so there's plenty to go around. Simply finish with an indulgent drizzle of icing sugar and maple syrup, and get ready for the culinary experience of a lifetime.

Hope you're ready now let's dive in!
