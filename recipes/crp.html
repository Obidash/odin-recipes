Cinnamon Roll Pancakes

Description

This recipe will take you through some steps to make some super delicious vegan cinnamon roll pancakes! If you have ever wanted some fluffly, mouth wateringly tasting, and aesthetically appealing pancakes, well then you're in for a treat!

This recipe makes 12 big pancakes, so there's plenty to go around. Simply finish with an indulgent drizzle of icing sugar and maple syrup, and enjoy the culinary experience of a lifetime.

Hope you're ready now let's dive in!

Ingredients

Pancake Mix

  • 500g plain flour
  • 2 tbsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 litre unsweetened almond milk
  • dairy-free butter, for frying

Cinnamon Mix

  • 120g dairy-free butter
  • 190g light brown sugar
  • 10g ground cinnamon

Maple Glaze Icing

  • 300g icing sugar
  • 50ml maple syrup, plus more for drizzling
  • 40ml water
