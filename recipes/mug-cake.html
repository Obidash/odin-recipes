Mug Cake

Description

What is a mug cake?

Now if you have never encounterd this delicous concoction you may be wondering what a mug cake even is. Don't worry I'm here to explain this delicously simple invention. The mug cake is a single serving cake that is made by putting some ingredients in a mug and thorwing it in the microwave. The best part about the mug cake is that it can be made in a manner of minutes!

So I hope you're ready to dive into this delectible desert! Don't worry it won't take too long!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil, melted coconut oil or vegan butter
  • 3 tablespoons non-dairy milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons dairy free chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Add the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, salt and baking powder to a mug. Stir or whisk to combine.
  2. Pour in the oil, non-dairy milk and vanilla into the mug with the dry ingredients and stir well with a spoon. Sprinkle the chocolate chips on top.
  3. Cook in the microwave for 40 seconds. It will likely be a little undercooked in the middle at this point, which is my preference, but if you want it cooked more, microwave it another 10-20 seconds.
  4. Serve immediately.
